Blender
AoE | Physical Skill
Akai fires a frog-shaped wave in the designated direction. The wave explodes upon hitting enemies or reaching its max travel distance, dealing 350–500 (+120% Extra Physical Attack) Physical Damage to enemies and marking them for 5 seconds.

Skill 2

Hurricane Dance
CC AoE | Physical Skill
Akai spins like a tornado for 3.5 seconds and knocks back the surrounding enemies, dealing 180–540 (+100% Extra Physical Attack) Physical Damage (Damage decays against the same target).
Using this skill removes all Debuffs from Akai immediately. He also becomes immune to Slowing Effect in the duration and increases 30 Movement Speed.
