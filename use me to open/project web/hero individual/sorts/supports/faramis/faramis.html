Vicious Retrieval
BUFF
When units die near Faramis, they leave their soul at the spot. Faramis recovers 150 (+60% Total Magic Power) points of HP every time he retrieves a soul. Each soul shortens Faramis' resurrecting time by 5% - 7% (scales with level).

Faramis can collect up to 18 souls and can shorten his resurrection time by up to 90%.

Shadow Stampede
BUFF CC
Faramis enters the Shadow State, increasing his Movement Speed by 70% as well as his Physical and Magical Defense by 50 / 60 / 70 / 80 / 90 / 100 points for 3 seconds, during which he is unable to  Basic Attack.

Faramis deals 325 / 370 / 415 / 460 / 505 / 550 (+60% Total Magic Power) points of Magic Damage to enemies that come into contact with him and leaves the Mark of Shadow if they're an enemy hero.

When he quits the Shadow State, all of the enemy heroes that have been marked will be pulled to Faramis and endure 325 / 370 / 415 / 460 / 505 / 550 (+60% Total Magic Power) points of Magic Damage.

Meanwhile. he restores 160 / 200 / 240 / 280 / 320 / 360 (+50%Total Magic Power) HP for every enemy hero pulled by this ability.
