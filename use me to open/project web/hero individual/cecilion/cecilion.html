Bats Feast
Burst Speed Up | Magic Skill
Cecilion unleashes Blood Demon power [for 6 seconds], increasing his Movement Speed by 60% (decaying rapidly over 1.5 seconds) and gaining immunity to slow effects. Meanwhile, he shoots 40 bolts of blood energy at the enemies nearby, each dealing 35–45 (+8% Total Magic Power) (+0.3% Total Mana) Magic Damage and slowing them by 3% (up to 30%) for 1 second.
Hitting an enemy heals him for 10 and 1% of his lost HP.
