Pride of Ice
BUFF
Each time her skill is cast, one unit of frost energy will be added to Aurora. When 3 units of frost energy are collected, the next damage of her skill will Freeze the target. Lasts 1.5 seconds.
Aurora deals 20% increased damage to targets in Frozen status with her skills.
